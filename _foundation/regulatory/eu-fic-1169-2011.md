# EU FIC 1169/2011 + 2021/382 — Food Information to Consumers

> Enforcement date: **Active** (in force since 2014; 2021/382 amendment active since 2022-02-01).
> PRD §11 states "Active" — `enforcement_date` above reflects today's review date as a proxy
> for an already-active regulation. No future enforcement date applies.
> Source URL: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:02011R1169-20180101
> 2021/382 amendment: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32021R0382

## Scope

EU Regulation 1169/2011 establishes the general principles, requirements and responsibilities
governing food information provided to consumers. Commission Regulation 2021/382 amends
Annex II (allergens) and Annex V (labelling exemptions).

**Key obligations:**
- Mandatory labelling of 14 major allergens (Annex II): gluten-containing cereals, crustaceans,
  eggs, fish, peanuts, soybeans, milk, nuts, celery, mustard, sesame, sulphites (≥10 mg/kg),
  lupin, molluscs.
- Allergen information must be clearly emphasised in the ingredients list (bold, italic, or underline).
- Nutrition declaration required on pre-packaged foods.
- Country of origin labelling for certain products (meat, fish, honey, fruits, vegetables).
- Minimum font size requirements for mandatory particulars.
- 2021/382 amendment: Specific requirements for allergen labelling in non-pre-packaged food (distance selling).

**Monopilot scope:**
- 01-NPD: 14 allergens tracked per PRD §11 ("14 alergenów EU FIC 1169/2011 (regulatory baseline)").
  `fg.*` events carry allergen change data (`fg.allergens_changed`).
- 11-SHIPPING: Labelling document generation with correct allergen emphasis and nutrition data.
